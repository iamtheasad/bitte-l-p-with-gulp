Future of Tourism Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a land of natural beauty, incredible history, seashores, world heritage sites, diverse wildlife etc. Despite being affected by COVID-19, Bangladesh is one of the countries that grasped the situation and opened tourism to everyone. According to WEF data, 97% of the tourists are locals, and there is still a lot of room for foreign tourists to visit Bangladesh.   Bangladesh tourism was held back due to a lack of convenient transportation, scarcity of travel information, lacklustre marketing strategies etc. BITTE is working to expand the horizons of Bangladesh’s tourism by overcoming its shortcomings. BITTE aims to nurture collaboration between local and international tourism companies to forge a clear path for the industry. Its experience in the industry will be essential in overcoming challenges and hurdles. Bangladesh is set to become a trending destination for both international and domestic tourists.

What is ATAB?

The Association of Travel Agents of Bangladesh, or ATAB, is a well-organised and popular association in Bangladesh. This association has around 3500 members all over the country and plays a vital role in the smoother development of the Aviation & Tourism sector in Bangladesh.

About ATAB

Atab Vision

Introducing tourism and aviation potentiality of Bangladesh Establishing the country as a tourist destination to the whole world.

Mission

Promote responsible air travel & tourism trade. Ensure welfare of member travel agencies. Support to development of tourism industry Generate skill manpower for tourism and travel agencies.

BITTE Overview

Bangladesh is the fastest socio economically growing country and Government of Bangladesh has taken many initiatives to promote travel & tourism. One of the mandates of ATAB is to expand the horizon of business opportunities in travel trade to and from Bangladesh. With this view in focus, ATAB works in tandem with the Tourism Ministry/Bodies not only in Bangladesh but also with other organization across the world to further the development of relations so that there is a mutual benefit for both countries. With the above objectives in mind, ATAB has embarked upon holding a mega event BITTE (Bangladesh International Travel and Tourism Expo) to be held in December 2022.

About BITTE

Bangladesh International Travel and Tourism Expo (BITTE) has designed to connect all stakeholders related to Travel and Tourism, offering the opportunity to exhibit in-person exhibition at The Expo covering - Airlines, Inbound, Outbound, Domestic, Culture & Heritage, Medical, M.I.C.E Tourism, Theme Parks, lifestyle, local and International Travel agencies and Tour Operators, Accommodation, Hajj, Umrah, Destination Management Companies-DMC , Tourist Transportation company, Cruise lines and so on. It’s an opportunity to showcase & sell products and services on the spot directly to B2B and B2C consumers. Also It is a platform for industry stakeholders to meet and connect with local & international travel trade partners.   ATAB, being the only association for the travel agencies always looking for the betterment of its members. It is also the largest & most active association working in the tourism sector in Bangladesh since 1976. The vibrant 3500 member travel agencies are the strength behind the success & pro activity of the association. However, present Executive Committee under the dynamic leadership of the President, Mr. S. N. Manzur Murshed (Mahbub) feels the urgency to arrange travel & tourism exhibition to connect the travel agents & tour operators around the world for better service to the clients.   ATAB take this event as an important & prestigious project for the development of the trade to strengthen the local tourism as well as extend support to Government to promote tourism in Bangladesh.
