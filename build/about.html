What is ATAB?

The Association of Travel Agents of Bangladesh, or ATAB, is a well-organised and popular association in Bangladesh. This association has around 3500 members all over the country and plays a vital role in the smoother development of the Aviation & Tourism sector in Bangladesh.

Atab Vision

Introducing tourism sectors of Bangladesh and establishing the country as a tourism-friendly to the whole world.

Mission

Development of Bangladesh’s tourism industry, the welfare of Travel Agencies (ATAB Members) and sustaining Air trafficking is the mission of ATAB.

Why Join ATAB?

After joining ATAB, the members will enjoy the following benefits:   *) Any member can avail of extensive support from ATAB to resolve any problem regarding the Ministry, Embassy & Airlines. ATAB support includes legal, technical and administrative as well.   *) ATAB Tourism Training Institute (ATTI) conducts training on Ticketing & Reservation. ATAB members’ staff are eligible for a 25% discount on this course fee.   *) Ministry of Civil Aviation Tourism (MoCAT) has made the obligation of being an ATAB member for license renewal purposes in the Ministry.   *) MoCAT has introduced training to Online Travel Agency Management System and ATAB members will get the opportunity to undertake this training. ATAB organizes different kinds of training in the ATTI and helps agency members to enhance professional skills.
